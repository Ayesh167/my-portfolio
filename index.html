01 — About

Systems, not scripts.

I'm an AI engineer focused on generative and agentic AI — building systems that reason, retrieve, and act, not just chat. My work sits at the intersection of model behavior and product: I build the retrieval pipelines and agent logic underneath, then the interface people actually use on top.

I work end-to-end — from RAG pipelines and agent orchestration in Python, to shipping the surrounding product in Next.js and TypeScript, with chatbots and AI assistants integrated as first-class features rather than an afterthought. Recent work follows a spec-driven development process: define the behavior precisely before writing a line of implementation, so the system does exactly what it was designed to do.

I'm looking for teams building real AI products — not demos — where agentic reasoning has to hold up in production.
